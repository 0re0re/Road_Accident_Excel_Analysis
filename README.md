### **2Ô∏è‚É£ Vehicles Most Involved**
- Cars accounted for **79.8%** of all casualties (333,485).  
- Motorcycles, buses, vans, and bicycles contributed much lower percentages.

### **3Ô∏è‚É£ Road Type Patterns**
- **Single carriageways** are the most common accident location (309K incidents).  
- Dual carriageways and roundabouts trail significantly behind.

### **4Ô∏è‚É£ Road Surface Conditions**
- Majority of accidents occurred on **dry surfaces** (279,445).  
- Wet surfaces accounted for **115,261** incidents.  
- Minimal cases were recorded on snowy/icy roads.

### **5Ô∏è‚É£ Light Conditions**
- Daylight conditions: **305K+** casualties

This shows that accidents remain significantly high even in clear visibility.

### **6Ô∏è‚É£ Urban vs Rural**
- Rural areas recorded **255.9K** casualties.  
- Urban areas recorded **162K**, showing **higher severity in rural regions**, despite lower traffic volume.
